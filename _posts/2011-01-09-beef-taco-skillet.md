The Ingredients

* 1lb ground beef
* 10.75oz can tomato soup
* 1/2c salsa
* 1/2c water
* 6 6" flour tortillas cut into 1" pieces
* 1/2c shredded cheddar cheese

The Steps

1. Brown beef
2. Strip in soup, salsa, water, tortillas and heat to a boil
3. Simmer for 5 minutes
4. Top with cheese
